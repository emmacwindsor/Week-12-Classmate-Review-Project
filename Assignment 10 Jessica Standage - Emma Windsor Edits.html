The Standage Family Band

Bluegrass Music From the Past...



Bio:

The Standage Family Band was founded in 2015, with the purchase of Jessica's banjo. As time passed, they welcomed a guitar, mandolin, and even a homemade washtub bass into the group! They take pride in singing 3-4 part harmonies, most of which are arranged by the band members themselves. Residing in Southen California, the Standage Family Band has been playing together for 4 years, always striving to bring back the honest mountain music from the past into today's busy culture. Sit back, and enjoy the bluegrass!
